About Migos

Migos, the iconic Atlanta-based hip-hop trio of Offset, Quavo, and Takeoff, revolutionized the trap genre with their signature triplet flow, infectious hooks, and chart-topping hits like Bad and Boujee and Stir Fry. Known for their dynamic chemistry and cultural influence, Migos became global superstars with the Culture album series, shaping modern hip-hop and leaving an indelible legacy in music and beyond"

Member Details

Offset

Kiari Kendrell Cephus, known professionally as Offset, is a founding member of Migos. Offset is known for his unique voice, intricate flows, and ad-libs that helped define the group’s trap sound. Beyond Migos, he has a successful solo career and collaborations with top artists.

Quavo

Quavious Keyate Marshall, better known as Quavo, is the frontman of Migos. Quavo's melodic hooks and rhythmic versatility have brought Migos chart-topping hits. As the most visible member, he bridges the group's sound with mainstream audiences.

Takeoff

Kirshnik Khari Ball, famously known as Takeoff, is the lyrical engine of Migos. His deep, introspective verses and smooth delivery have earned him acclaim as one of the most talented rappers of his generation. Takeoff has left a lasting legacy through his unmatched creativity.
